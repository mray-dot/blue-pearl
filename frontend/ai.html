AI Governance

Governance that makes AI trustworthy.

Help leaders deploy AI responsibly, with risk controls, policy oversight, and human accountability.

Why this training matters

AI can create legal, ethical, privacy, and operational risks — governance mitigates these through policy and oversight.

Core modules

  • AI foundations
  • Laws and standards
  • Ethics-by-design
  • Testing & validation
  • Deployment monitoring

Learning outcomes

Set governance policies, assess AI risk, review model use, monitor deployment, and make human-in-the-loop decisions.

Governance tools

Risk register, model inventory, approval workflow, policy controls, audit logs, vendor assessment.
