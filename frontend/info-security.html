Information Security

Protecting information with confidence, discipline, and care.

Learn how Blue Pearl helps teams handle sensitive data responsibly, reduce risk, and strengthen confidentiality across daily operations.

Why this training matters

Phishing, malware, access misuse, social engineering, and data leakage are everyday business risks; this course explains them in simple, actionable terms.

Core modules

  • Information security basics
  • Threats and attacks
  • Access and authentication
  • Personal data protection and privacy
  • Incident reporting

Learning outcomes

  • Identify risks
  • Apply secure handling practices
  • Protect personal and organizational data
  • Respond to incidents correctly

Policy principles

  • Least privilege
  • Strong passwords & MFA
  • Encrypted storage & secure sharing
  • Clean desk / clean screen
